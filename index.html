Trail Tracker App

I love to mountain bike, whether its cross country biking or downhill biking if it has two wheels and some pedals count me in!

I often use the Strava app and the MTB Project app to find my next adventures. So this inspired me to build Trail-Tracker.

It's an app that allows trail enthusiasts to post information and keep track of their favorite trails. Users can check out other riders trails, rate the trails and post useful information that can help future riders.

Technologies used: Google OAuth, JavaScript, EJS, HTML, CSS, Mongoose, MongoDB, Node.js, Express.js

Sudz Love App

Sudz Love can have so many meanings but in this scenario we are talking about beer enthusiasts!

Have you ever taken that first sip of a new brew and realized its so delicious that you wish you could tell everyone about it? This project was inspired by the Untappd app.

Create a profile, login, tell us a little bit about the beer and post a picture with the details. Its a community wall where like minded beer lovers can share their favorites and you can also search for breweries near by.

Technologies used: React, JavaScript, EJS, HTML, CSS, Mongoose, MongoDB, Node.js, Express.js Open Brewery DB API

About Me

Hello my name is Adam. I’ve lived in sunny Colorado almost my entire life. Throughout my time I find myself moving closer to the mountains where I can enjoy the beauty of the outdoors. I was an automotive technician at Mercedes Benz for the past 7 years. The most notable part of my day to day was vehicle diagnostics because I found the challenges that come with problem solving to be rewarding. More importantly I enjoy the satisfaction a customer would get from having their vehicle repaired properly. During the last year of my career I was offered the shop Forman position. At that time I realized that my career had reached its peak and my joy for problem solving would soon plateau.

I made the bold move leaving my career in the rear view and went back to school for a Computer Science degree. I am currently a Senior at Metropolitan State University of Denver with one semester left before gradtuating with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science. While continuing to be a full time student I enrolled in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Program to accelerate by skill growth and to hone in on the Software Development field. Overall, I’m excitedly looking forward to the unknown and endless possibilities this new career will bring to my future!
